About Us

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is one of the most exciting and passionate teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2012, SRH has built a reputation for strong bowling attacks, fearless batting, and a never-give-up attitude. With a dedicated fan base known as the Orange Army, SRH continues to shine in the IPL with determination and spirit.
